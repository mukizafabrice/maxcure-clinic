About Max Cure Clinic

A Kigali clinic built on a simple belief: every family deserves compassionate, high-quality care.

Our Story

From a Small Practice to a Trusted Name

Founded in 2018 by Dr. NIYIBIZI Elie, Max Cure Clinic was born from a simple belief: every family in Kigali deserves access to compassionate, high-quality medical care regardless of their background.

What began as a pediatric practice has grown into a full multi-specialty clinic serving thousands of patients annually — adding general medicine, dentistry, a modern laboratory, and gynecology along the way.

Today, Max Cure stands as a place where neighbours are treated like family, where doors stay open seven days a week, and where excellent medicine meets genuine warmth.

What Drives Us

Mission, Vision & Values

Our Mission

To provide accessible, compassionate, and high-quality healthcare to every family in Kigali — without compromise.

We measure our success not in numbers, but in the trust patients place in us, visit after visit.

Our Vision

To be Rwanda's most trusted community clinic — a model for warm, modern, multi-specialty care.

We see a future where excellent medicine is the standard, not the exception, in every neighbourhood.

Our Values

Compassion, integrity, and respect guide every interaction — from the front desk to the consultation room.

We treat each patient as a whole person, with dignity and honesty at the centre of their care.

Founder & Medical Director

Dr. NIYIBIZI Elie

Dr. NIYIBIZI Elie is a pediatrics specialist and the founding force behind Max Cure Clinic. With more than a decade of clinical experience, he has dedicated his career to making excellent healthcare both accessible and deeply human for the families of Kigali.

After training and practising across Rwanda, Dr. Niyibizi opened Max Cure in 2018 with a single examination room and an unwavering principle: treat every patient as you would treat your own family. That principle has shaped the clinic's culture as it grew into a multi-specialty practice.

Today he leads a dedicated team of clinicians, balancing his role as medical director with the work he loves most — caring directly for children and their parents.

"My commitment is simple — every child and every patient who walks through our doors will receive the same care I would give my own family."
