Good to Know

Frequently Asked Questions

Please bring your valid insurance card and a national ID or passport. Having these ready helps us verify your coverage quickly so you can be seen without delay.
For most services and most insurers, no referral is needed — you can book directly with us. A few specialist plans may require pre-authorisation; our reception team will let you know if that applies to you.
Coverage depends on your specific plan. Many plans cover consultations, laboratory tests and treatment in full, while some include a small co-payment. We'll always confirm the details with you before any treatment begins.
You are very welcome as a self-pay patient. Our rates are transparent and fair, and our reception team can explain the cost of any service before you commit.
Verification is typically completed within a few minutes at reception. To save even more time, you can let us know your provider when you book your appointment online.
