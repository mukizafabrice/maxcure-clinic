Our Medical Services

Five specialties, one trusted clinic. Comprehensive, compassionate care for every member of your family in Kigali.

Specialty Care

Pediatrics

Children are not small adults — they need clinicians who understand their unique needs. Our pediatric team provides warm, attentive care from the first days of life through adolescence, in an environment designed to put both child and parent at ease.

From routine wellness visits and immunizations to the diagnosis and treatment of childhood illnesses, we monitor your child's growth and development at every stage. Early detection and gentle treatment are at the heart of what we do.

When your child is unwell, every hour matters. Because we are open seven days a week, you never have to wait for a weekday to get expert help for fever, infections, or sudden illness.

Conditions We Treat

  • Malaria & fevers
  • Respiratory infections
  • Diarrhea & dehydration
  • Routine immunizations
  • Growth & nutrition concerns
  • Skin & allergic conditions
  • Newborn check-ups
  • Developmental screening

What to Expect

  1. Friendly check-inOur reception verifies your details and insurance in minutes.
  2. Gentle examinationA pediatric specialist assesses your child and explains every step.
  3. Clear care planYou leave with medication, tests, or a follow-up plan you understand.

Specialty Care

General Medicine

Our general medicine department is the first point of care for adults across Kigali. Whether you have an acute illness, a chronic condition, or simply want a thorough check-up, our physicians take the time to listen and treat the whole person.

We manage common and complex conditions alike — from infections and digestive complaints to long-term care for hypertension and diabetes. With our in-house laboratory, diagnosis and treatment happen under one roof, without delay.

Preventive medicine is a cornerstone of our practice. Regular screening and honest health advice help you stay well, rather than only treating illness once it appears.

Conditions We Treat

  • Hypertension
  • Diabetes management
  • Respiratory infections
  • Malaria & typhoid
  • Digestive disorders
  • General check-ups
  • Preventive screening
  • Minor procedures

What to Expect

  1. ConsultationA physician reviews your history and symptoms in detail.
  2. On-site testingAny necessary lab work is done immediately in our laboratory.
  3. Treatment & follow-upYou receive a tailored plan and a clear path for any next steps.

Specialty Care

Dentistry

A healthy smile is part of a healthy life. Our dental service combines modern equipment with a calm, reassuring approach, so even nervous patients feel comfortable in the chair.

We offer the full range of everyday dental care — professional cleanings, fillings, extractions, and treatment for pain and infection. Our focus is always on preserving your natural teeth wherever possible.

Good oral health starts with prevention. We take time to show patients of all ages how to care for their teeth at home, reducing problems before they begin.

Conditions We Treat

  • Tooth decay & cavities
  • Gum disease
  • Toothache & infection
  • Professional cleaning
  • Fillings & restorations
  • Extractions
  • Teeth whitening
  • Children's dentistry

What to Expect

  1. Oral assessmentThe dentist examines your teeth and gums and discusses any concerns.
  2. Comfortable treatmentProcedures are explained and carried out gently, with pain kept to a minimum.
  3. Care adviceYou receive personalised guidance to keep your smile healthy at home.

Specialty Care

Laboratory

Accurate testing is the foundation of good medicine. Our modern, on-site laboratory delivers reliable results quickly — so your doctor can make confident decisions without delay.

From routine blood counts and malaria tests to biochemistry and infection screening, our trained technicians follow strict quality standards on every sample. Most common results are ready the same day.

Because the lab is part of the clinic, there is no need to travel elsewhere or wait days for answers. Your test, diagnosis, and treatment can all happen in a single visit.

Tests We Offer

  • Full blood count
  • Malaria testing
  • Blood sugar & lipids
  • Liver & kidney function
  • Urinalysis
  • Pregnancy testing
  • Infection screening
  • Stool analysis

What to Expect

  1. Sample collectionA technician collects your sample carefully and comfortably.
  2. Rapid analysisYour sample is processed on-site under strict quality control.
  3. Same-day resultsMost results are shared with your doctor the very same day.

Specialty Care

Gynecology

Women's health deserves privacy, respect, and genuine expertise. Our gynecology service offers a safe, confidential space where women of every age feel heard and cared for.

We provide routine examinations, screening, family planning advice, and management of common gynecological conditions. For expectant mothers, we offer attentive antenatal support throughout pregnancy.

Every consultation is handled with discretion and compassion. We believe informed women make empowered choices about their bodies and their health.

Conditions We Treat

  • Routine well-woman exams
  • Antenatal care
  • Family planning
  • Menstrual disorders
  • Infections & screening
  • Cervical cancer screening
  • Menopause support
  • Fertility guidance

What to Expect

  1. Private consultationYou speak with a specialist in a confidential, judgment-free setting.
  2. Careful examinationAny examination or screening is done with your comfort and dignity first.
  3. Personalised planYou receive clear advice and a care plan that fits your life and needs.
